Industry Partners

Our Vision

The UCL AI Centre is based in the heart of London and is one of the largest academic AI research centres in the UK, being home to over 100 researchers. The Centre is part of the Department of Computer Science, which was classed by the UK government as the UK’s leading Computer Science Department in research.

The AI Centre was formed in 2019 from a 20-year tradition of AI research and teaching at UCL and is recognised for its research excellence (including the DeepMind Chair in AI) and commitment to AI for good (including the UNESCO Chair in AI). The Centre regularly publishes at leading AI conferences and contributes to social projects around the globe, including AI for healthcare and free AI education. The Centre also acts as an engine for innovation, with academics and students having created companies with a total valuation to date of over $100 million, supporting economic and social development.

The Industry Partner Programme is designed to enable connections between industry and the Centre. At its heart, the Centre aims to make the world a better place through Research, Innovation and Training. However, we can't do this alone and the Programme is an opportunity to help us and our Partners achieve common goals. Membership of the programme is annual (except for multi-year components).


What we can offer

The Programme is structured around five main areas:

  • Network: get to know students at the Centre
  • Insight: understand the Centre’s research interests and results
  • Connect: bespoke consulting and training by pairing with individual academics
  • Fellowships: engage jointly in research by sponsoring a Fellow
  • PhD Student Sponsorship: engage with a research student over 4 years
